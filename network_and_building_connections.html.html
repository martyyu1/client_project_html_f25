Networking & Building Connections

Welcome to Networking & Building Connections

Building a strong professional network is one of the most valuable steps you can take in your UMSI journey and beyond. This page is your launchpad to make meaningful connections—whether you're reaching out to peers, alumni, employers, or mentors.

Here, you'll find not just a list of resources, but guided pathways. Dive into the essentials, get answers to your top questions, and discover tools tailored to your goals. Use the featured topics below to start where you feel most comfortable, and don't hesitate to explore new ways to grow your network!

FAQs

  • How do I start networking if I'm new to it?
    Begin with people you know—classmates, professors, or alumni. Attend events and practice introducing yourself.
  • How do I find UMSI alumni to reach out to?
    Use Career Link and University Career Alumni Network (UCAN) to search by role, company, or area of interest.
  • Are there workshops or coaching for networking skills?
    Yes! Visit our Events & Workshops page or schedule a coaching session to get personalized support.
  • What if I'm nervous about reaching out to strangers?
    Remember that most people are open to helping others. Prepare your questions in advance and focus on building genuine relationships.
